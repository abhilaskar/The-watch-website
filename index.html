Our Heritage

Seven Decades of
Uncompromised
Excellence


Founded in 1947 in the heart of Geneva, Chronos was born from a singular obsession: to create timepieces that transcend function and enter the realm of art. Every watch that leaves our atelier carries three generations of accumulated wisdom.

We source only the finest materials — from conflict-free diamonds to hand-selected sapphire crystals — and each movement is assembled under 5x magnification by artisans with decades of dedicated experience.

The Chronos Difference

Engineered for Eternity

01

Swiss Precision Movement

Each calibre is hand-assembled by master horologists and certified to COSC standards — accurate to ±2 seconds per day across any condition or altitude.

02

Sapphire Crystal Glass

Double-sided anti-reflective sapphire — hardness 9 on Mohs scale — offers unmatched clarity and scratch resistance that endures across decades of daily wear.

03

300M Water Resistance

Triple-sealed crowns and pressure-tested cases engineered to reach 300 meters — from the ocean floor to the boardroom with absolute, unwavering elegance.

04

Italian Calfskin Straps

Each strap is hand-stitched from Grade-A Italian calfskin leather — aged naturally for suppleness, dyed with traditional vegetable tannins for depth of character.
